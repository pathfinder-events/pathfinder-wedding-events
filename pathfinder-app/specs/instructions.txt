MANUAL COCKTAIL & WINE SPECIFICATIONS FOLDER
===========================================

You can upload your cocktail and wine specification PDFs, text files, or Markdown files directly into this directory (`/public/specs/`).

### How It Works:
1. Place any of your event specifications, brand sheets, or cocktail recipe PDFs right here in this folder.
2. When you trigger the 'Sync' action in the header of the Pathfinder app, the local backend will scan this folder, parse the contents of your PDFs/text files, and seamlessly feed that specific premium knowledge into the Navigator's brain.
3. The Navigator will then be able to reference the exact ingredients, ratios, garnishes, and wine notes from your files, citing them with direct, working links to these files (e.g., `/specs/your-file.pdf`).

### Supported Formats:
- **PDF Files (`.pdf`)**: Parsed natively on sync to extract up to 12,000 characters of text.
- **Text Files (`.txt` / `.md`)**: Parsed directly to read recipe text.
